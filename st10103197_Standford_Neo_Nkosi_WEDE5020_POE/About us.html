Our Story

Sweet Vibes Café by Astrid is a small, family-run home-based café and bakery founded in 2023 by Astrid Mathilda Marques in Brackenfell, Western Cape. Starting as a modest home kitchen operation at 20 Goud cres, brackenfell, Astrid began baking and selling homemade treats at local flea markets during the post-COVID period.

Our Mission Statement

To create sweet moments and spread good vibes by serving fresh, homemade baked goods and quality coffee with warmth, care, and excellent service to our local community.

Our Vision Statement

To become Brackenfell’s favourite community café, known for exceptional homemade treats and a welcoming space that brings people together, while expanding into a beloved local brand that inspires joy and connection.

Our Staff

The friendly and attentive staff at Sweet Vibes Café bring warm smiles and great energy to every visit. Efficient, helpful, and genuinely welcoming, they’re the perfect match for the café’s relaxed and sweet atmosphere.
